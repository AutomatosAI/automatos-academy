# AI Business Foundations (ABF) — Free training for business operators

**Learn what AI actually is and run your business with it — no jargon, no hype, no exam.** ABF is
the academy's **Operator lane** flagship: for owners, founders, team leads, and non-technical
professionals who keep hearing about AI and can't yet see where it fits *their* business. Every
module answers one real business question and ends with a **🛠 Do it now** task on the free
Automatos platform. Blueprint: [PRD-B1-AI-BUSINESS.md](../../prds/PRD-B1-AI-BUSINESS.md).

> **Plain English first, term second — always.** ("Teach the AI your business — this is called
> RAG.") No acronym before its plain-language job. No selling: the platform pitch is the hands-on
> experience, never the copy. Recurring worked examples: a café, a marketing agency, an
> e-commerce shop, a consultancy.

## What makes this track different

- **No exam, no grade, no gate.** Progress = modules done + the capstone. Optional knowledge
  checks are ungated self-assessment.
- **Artifacts, not certificates of attendance.** By the end the learner owns: an AI opportunity
  map, a delegation/prompt library, a grounded assistant on their own documents, one automated
  workflow, a one-page AI policy, a cost/ROI one-pager, and a one-page AI operating plan.
- **Completion = "AI-operational"** — they shipped one real automation in their own business.
  Badge per [PRD-CREDENTIALS.md](../../prds/PRD-CREDENTIALS.md).
- **Honest bridge:** want a real credential after? → the S4 **Gen-AI-Leader** exam sub-track
  ($99, business-audience — [PRD-S4](../../prds/PRD-S4-CROSS-VENDOR.md) §7). Want platform
  depth? → [APA](../automatos-apa/).

## The workflow — do this per module

1. Open the module file (e.g. `03-teach-it-your-business.md`).
2. In NotebookLM, create a notebook and **add the sources** the file lists — nothing extra.
3. **Video:** paste the *Video* prompt → Customize → generate → tune to **~6–8 min** (shorter
   than cert tracks — this audience won't sit twelve minutes).
4. **Audio:** paste the *Deep Dive* prompt (every module) and the *Debate* prompt (01, 05, 07).
5. **Do it:** complete the **🛠 Do it now** task on the free platform — that's the real learning.
6. Download the media → host → register in the track's `videos[]`. Tick the module off below.

## Media per module

- **1 × Video** (~6–8 min) — advisor tone, one worked business example, zero hype.
- **Audio:** **Deep Dive** (every module) + **Debate** on the judgment modules (01 quick-wins vs
  transformation · 05 how much autonomy · 07 where's the ROI, honestly).
- **No exam Briefs** — there is no exam.
